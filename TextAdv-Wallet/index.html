Life On The Streets

By: Geri Kona

The objective of this... wondrous adventure is to make it to the end (go figure) by overcoming all the obstacles (reading) on your path.

Delve deep into your mind, soul, and gun box and gather up the courage to face what lies behind... The Scary Door.

And remember, the secret is believing in yourself...
and not dying, that's bad.



Lets begin!

You are walking down a street all by your lonesome.
It occurs to you that you've forgotten where you were going in the first place. As you stand there, you notice a wallet before your feet. The streets appear earily silent as you scan your surrounding for the owner of the wallet or police officers.

..."Pick it up already!", the thought echoes in your mind over and over again, as if a greater power is attempting to pass great wisdom directly to you...

You decide to pick the wallet up off the ground.

You ignore the clear signs from the universe and continue walking.

Retreat!

You just washed your hands so you decide to move along... CRASH!

As the light fades, you wonder to youself what you just experienced. You finally begin to snap out of it and get a sense of your surroundings. No longer standing on a city sidewalk, you find yourself in a completely unrecognizable environment.

You notice off in the distance a cluster of strangly shaped structures surounded by nothing but sand and rocks.
